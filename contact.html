Good to Know
Common questions
Is your jewellery real gold?
Yes — every piece is authentic gold, weighed in front of you on video if you wish. We are happy to provide weight and karat details for any item before you order.
Do you ship internationally?
We ship worldwide — the UK, Canada, USA, UAE, Australia and more. International orders are insured and tracked door-to-door. Shipping cost and timing are confirmed on WhatsApp before you pay.
How do I pay?
We accept bank transfer within Pakistan and international transfer options for overseas customers. Full payment details are shared securely on WhatsApp when you order.
Can I request a custom piece?
Absolutely. Send us a reference photo on WhatsApp and we'll advise on design, weight and price. Custom orders take a little longer — we'll give you a clear timeline upfront.
