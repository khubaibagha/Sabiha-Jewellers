Prong Repair & Re-Tipping
Worn, broken, or missing prongs are a leading cause of stone loss. We re-tip and rebuild prongs to secure your diamonds and gemstones before it's too late.
Chain & Necklace Repair
Snapped or kinked chains, broken clasps, and detached pendants — we solder, clasp, and restore necklaces and chains of all types and metals.
Bracelet Repair
Broken links, failed clasps, bent bangles — we repair bracelets in gold, silver, platinum, and fashion metals with fast, invisible results.
Earring Repair
Broken posts, bent wires, missing backs, and damaged hoops — we repair and restore earrings of all types and sizes, including fine and costume pieces.
Stone Re-Setting
Loose or fallen stones reset securely. We re-set diamonds, rubies, sapphires, emeralds, and all other gemstones in any metal type.
Professional Cleaning
Ultrasonic and steam cleaning restores brilliance and sparkle. Free with every repair — also available as a standalone service. Walk-ins welcome.
Clasp Replacement
A broken clasp makes a necklace or bracelet unwearable. We replace clasps quickly with a proper, secure fitting that matches the original style.
Jewelry Repurposing
Old or unwanted jewelry transformed into something you'll love to wear again. We repurpose stones, metals, and settings into new custom designs.
Shank Replacement
A worn or cracked ring shank can be replaced entirely — restoring structural integrity and giving an old ring a brand new life.

Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does jewelry repair take?
Most common repairs — chain soldering, clasp replacement, prong re-tipping, stone setting — are completed within a few business days. More complex work may take longer. We'll give you an honest timeframe when you bring your piece in.
How much does jewelry repair cost?
Repair costs vary by the type of work, the metal, and the complexity of your piece. We always provide a free, honest quote before starting any work. You'll know the exact cost before we touch your jewelry.
Can you repair antique or heirloom jewelry?
Yes — we welcome antique and heirloom pieces and treat them with the extra care they deserve. We assess the piece carefully before recommending any repair approach and will always discuss options with you first.
My stone fell out — can you find and reset it?
If you still have the stone, we can re-set it. If the stone is lost, we can source a matching replacement — just bring in the piece so we can match the size, shape, and quality. We work with diamonds, colored gemstones, and synthetic stones.
Do you repair fashion or costume jewelry?
It depends on the piece and the repair needed. Some fashion jewelry can be repaired; others may not be worth the cost. Bring it in and we'll give you an honest assessment and quote.
