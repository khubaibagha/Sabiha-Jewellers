Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
How much does custom jewelry cost?
Custom jewelry spans a wide price range depending on the metal, stone choice, complexity of the design, and time involved. A simple custom pendant may start at a few hundred dollars; a fully custom diamond engagement ring can range from $1,000 to $10,000+. We'll work within your budget and help you get the most value from your investment.
How long does the custom design process take?
The timeline depends on the complexity of your piece and current studio workload. Simple designs may be completed in 1-2 weeks; more complex pieces can take 3-6 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your consultation and keep you updated throughout.
Can I bring in my own stone?
Absolutely — we love working with stones that have personal significance. Whether it's a family diamond, a gemstone you sourced yourself, or a stone from an older piece you want to repurpose, we can build a custom setting designed specifically for your stone.
What if I don't know exactly what I want?
That's exactly what our consultation is for. Many of our guests come in with just a general idea — "something vintage-inspired" or "a simple solitaire but different." We ask the right questions, show examples, and help you discover exactly what you're looking for. There's no pressure and no obligation at the consultation stage.
Can you redesign or repurpose existing jewelry?
Yes — repurposing is one of our most meaningful services. If you have jewelry sitting unworn in a drawer — grandmother's ring, an old necklace, a dated bracelet — we can melt down the metal, extract the stones, and design something you'll wear proudly every day.
