Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does ring resizing take?
Most standard ring resizes are completed within a few days. More complex resizes — such as platinum rings, rings with stones around the band, or rings requiring major size changes — may take longer. We'll give you an honest turnaround estimate when you come in.
How much does ring resizing cost?
Pricing depends on the metal type, how many sizes the ring needs to change, and the ring's design. We always provide a free, upfront quote before starting any work — there are no hidden fees.
Can you resize a ring with stones around the band (eternity ring)?
Eternity rings and channel-set bands present a challenge since the stones go all the way around. We can often size these rings slightly using specialized techniques. We'll evaluate your specific ring and give you an honest assessment of what's possible.
How many sizes can you go up or down?
We can typically resize rings up or down by up to 4-5 sizes, depending on the ring's original design and metal. Very large size changes may affect the ring's proportions. We'll advise you honestly on what works best for your piece.
Will the resize be visible?
When done correctly, a ring resize should be invisible. Our craftsmen meticulously blend the metal and polish every seam so the join cannot be seen. We then clean the entire ring to bring back its original luster.
Do you resize tungsten, titanium, or stainless steel rings?
These metals (tungsten, titanium, stainless steel, cobalt) cannot be resized by traditional methods due to their extreme hardness. If you have a ring in one of these metals, please bring it in and we can discuss your options — including replacement in some cases.
