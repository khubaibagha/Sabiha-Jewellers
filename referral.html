Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer?
No limit at all. Refer as many friends, family members, or coworkers as you'd like — you'll earn a $25 credit for each one who completes a visit.
What services qualify for the friend discount?
The 15% discount applies to repairs, ring resizing, and professional cleaning. Custom design and bridal consultations are already free — ask us in-store about how referral credit can apply toward a custom piece.
How do I redeem my $25 credit?
Just let us know on your next visit. Once your friend has been in and mentioned your name or code, we'll apply your $25 credit toward whatever service you need.
Does my friend need to bring anything?
No — they just need to mention your name or the code JOURNEY15 when they check in for their first visit.
