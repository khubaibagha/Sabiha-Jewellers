Where It Began
Built in
Gul Plaza

Sabiha Jewellery began in Gul Plaza — Karachi's beloved home of gold. For years, women came to us for pieces that marked their milestones: engagements, weddings, Eid, the birth of a daughter, a promotion earned.

We learned what Pakistani women want from their gold — beauty that lasts, weight that holds value, and designs that feel like their own.

Today, sabihas lives online. Through Instagram and WhatsApp we serve a community of over 36,000 customers — in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and across the world from London to Toronto to Dubai. Every piece is photographed honestly, priced fairly, and shipped with care to wherever you call home.
